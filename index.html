HCI Design Manifesto

How the Design Process Affects Project Goals and Completion

Written by John Amaral


When designing any product with at least one user in mind, it is important to not jump the gun and dive head-first into the actual designing phase. Any form of design, but especially human-centered design, requires brainstorming, prototyping, and testing, all of which need to be iterated upon multiple times to end up with a satisfying final product.
